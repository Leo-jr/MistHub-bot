Privacy Policy for Temp VC Bot

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Temp VC Bot collects and stores the following data to provide its service:

  • Guild (Server) IDs: To identify which servers have the bot installed
  • Channel IDs: To manage temporary voice channels and generator channels
  • User IDs: To track voice channel ownership, trusted users, and access permissions
  • Voice Channel Settings: Name, user limit, bitrate, privacy settings (locked/hidden status)
  • Access Lists: Permitted and denied user IDs for each voice channel

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Create and manage temporary voice channels
  • Enforce voice channel permissions and access control
  • Maintain voice channel settings and configurations
  • Display ownership and trusted user information

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in JSON files on our server. We do not use third-party databases or services to store your data.

4. Data Retention

  • Voice channel data is automatically deleted when the channel is removed or empty
  • Guild configuration is retained as long as the bot remains in your server
  • All data is deleted when the bot is removed from your server

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties. Your data remains private and is used exclusively for bot functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data by removing the bot from your server
  • Access information about what data we store
  • Opt-out by not using the bot

7. Children's Privacy

Our bot does not knowingly collect information from users under 13. Discord's Terms of Service require users to be 13 or older.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our support server or email.
